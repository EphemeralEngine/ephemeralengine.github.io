# About Me

Henry Stover is a designer, illustrator, and (arts educator)[/teaching] currently based in Maryland, but originally from North Carolina. He likes to create mixed media and sequential art. He studied Art and Design with a concentration in Animation and a minor in Computer Science at NC State University. His favorite things to make live at the intersection of art and technology with a focus on human storytelling.

Lately, he has been focused on illustrating a beastiary for a tabletop RPG, doing layouts for a comic book, and creating animatics just for fun. 

He is redesigning his website now, so hopefully, you will be able to see more of his artwork soon!
