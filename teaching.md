# Media Arts Classes

I have worked in Art and Design education outside of traditional classrooms for the past 5 years with a focus on teaching teens fundamental media arts skills.

I believe that everything is a learned skill and talent is much less important than a willingness to learn, fail, and try again. 
My teaching style focuses on giving students a place to explore and create without judgement in order to get a crash course in software that might seem intimidating at first.
The breadth of mediums I have explored lends well to this - I have taught workshops on a variety of topics such as intros to 3D modeling in Blender, digital photo editing, digital illustration, and branding fundamentals.

If you're in the DMV area and interested in hiring me to teach a class, don't hesitate to [reach out](/contact).
